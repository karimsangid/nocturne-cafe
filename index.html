Premise01

Not a coffee shop with a quiet corner. A reading room that happens to pour coffee.

stay as long
as you need →

Everywhere else dims the lights at nine. We open at six and keep going. Nocturne is built for 17–23 year-olds who actually need to get work done — and want somewhere calm, warm, and unhurried to do it.

01

Quiet by rule

Indoor voices, headphones not speakers, calls taken outside. The stillness of a library, on purpose.

02

Built to linger

An outlet at every seat, fast free Wi-Fi, and nobody circling your table. Refills are a dollar so you'll stay.

03

Open absurdly late

3am weeknights, 5am weekends. When the deadline's at nine and the library shut at eleven — this room is still lit.

House Rules05

A short list that keeps it calm.

Headphones, not speakers

Your soundtrack stays in your ears. The room stays quiet for everyone.

The seat is yours

No time limits, no table police. Settle in for the whole night if you like.

Calls happen outside

Quick step out for the phone. Your table will be exactly where you left it.

Everyone's welcome

Students, night owls, early-shift workers. If you need a calm room, you've found it.
